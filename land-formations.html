Beaches In The Bohol Paradise

The province’s coastline is full of gentle and beautiful coves perfect for anyone to appreciate the beauty of nature. It’s also internationally known as a top destination for divers and snorkelers because the aquatic life below is pristine and abundant. Moreover, whale and dolphin watching is also popular among tourists especially from March to June! The best part about all this is that some of the beaches you may find are barely touched thanks to the impeccable policies and protection provided by the resorts and the local government alike.

Alona Beach Resort

Alona beach is one of the emerging top travel destinations in the Philippines because of countless reasons. First, this small stretch of tropical paradise features a beautiful white sand beach, crystal clear and blue waters, a mesmerising view of the sunset, and tons of exciting tours and activities for everyone! The second is that its convenient location enables tourists to visit some of Bohol’s top destinations such as the Chocolate Hills, Loboc River, and the Tarsier Sanctuary. Third, countless hotels and resorts are scattered all over Alona Beach which are all offering to accommodate you during one of your most memorable vacations ever. Lastly, Alona Beach resides in the ever-famous Panglao Island, where you can find some of the most luxurious resorts and attractions ever in the entire country.

Make sure to make the most out of your close encounter with these beautiful sea creatures!
